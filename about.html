About me

Hello! I'm Zanele, a dedicated Computer Science student with a passion for leveraging technology to solve real-world problems. Currently pursuing my degree at IU International University of Applied Science, I have embarked on a thrilling journey into the world of computer science, driven by my innate curiosity and a desire to make a meaningful impact through innovation.

Whether it's through developing cutting-edge software or championing cybersecurity measures, I am committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the digital age.
